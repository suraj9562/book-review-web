ABOUT US

One web page for every book ever published. It's a lofty but achievable goal.

To build Open Library, we need hundreds of millions of book records, a wiki interface, and lots of people who are willing to contribute their time and effort to building the site.

To date, we have gathered over 20 million records from a variety of large catalogs as well as single contributions, with more on the way.

Open Library is an open project: the software is open, the data are open, the documentation is open, and we welcome your contribution. Whether you fix a typo, add a book, or write a widget--it's all welcome. We have a small team of fantastic programmers who have accomplished a lot, but we can't do it alone!

Open Library is a project of the non-profit Internet Archive, and has been funded in part by a grant from the Library Of Vishwakarma Info. Tech. Pune.

Our Goal

Access to knowledge, promote scholarly communication and provide information to community in support to research and instructional programs.

Leadership in information policy development, information applications and information research.

Engage the community in developing and promoting resources and partnership opportunities.

Provide a versatile, welcoming, collaborative, and safe space that serves as an anchor for the community.
